Pick Your Trail See Narmada As Per Your Interest

Marble Rocks

Experience the stunning marble cliffs rising to a height of 100 feet on either side of Narmada River. This spectacular natural wonder creates a magical gorge that gleams in moonlight.

Dhuandhar Falls

Witness the smoky cascade of water falling from a height of 98 feet. The misty appearance created by the fine water particles gives it the name 'Dhuandhar'.

Chausath Yogini Temple

Explore this ancient temple dedicated to 64 Yoginis, built in circular fashion on a hilltop. Experience the architectural marvel dating back to the 10th century.
